I am an Electrical Engineering major at Brigham Young University, with minors in Mathematics and Business. Despite being kept busy by that and any current employment I greatly enjoy being outdoors no matter the season. I love hiking in the summer, skiing in the winter, and everything else in between. If im not working at a desk I am most likely trying to get people together for some activity or game.

Skiing in the Mountains And other past times

My favorite past time by far is skiing in the mountains. Growing up about an hour away from New York City, there were not many opportunities to try alpine skiing, and never really enjoying sports based off of endurance, this area of sports remained closed to me until a group of friends in college dragged me along. I fell in love instantly.

Canines of all varieties have always intriguied me. When I was younger I always wanted to be a K-9 unit police officer. This desire eventually faded the further I learned about science, and eventually changed to engineering. While studying electrical engineering I find all disciplines interesting and try to spend some free time learning about them in ways like combining arduino projects with mechanical systems for mechatronics, and 3d printing/cad to make these parts.

My favorite games include Dungeons and Dragons, Settlers of Cattan, and Zelda: Breath of the Wild.
